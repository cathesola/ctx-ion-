Analysis of Heavy Metals

Category : Pollutant Analysis

Sub-Category: Heavy Metals

Sub-Category Information: Heavy Metals are metallic elements that have a high density and atomic weight. Some common examples of heavy metals include lead, mercury, cadmium, arsenic, and nickel. These elements are naturally occurring in the environment, but they can also be released into the environment as a result of human activities such as industrial processes, mining, and agricultural practices.

Paramenters : Ca, As, Be, Cd, Cr, Mn, Zn, Cu, Pb, Ni, Se, V, Na, K, Mg, U, Th, Tl, Fe, Ag, Al, Co, Ba

ICP-OES : Instrument

Minimum Sample Amount : 10g

Sample Type : Soil, Water, Sediment, Biological Tissue and Foods(non-oily)
