Analysis of Volatile Organic Compounds in Various Matrices

Category : Pollutant Analysis

Sub-Category: VOC

Sub-Category Information: Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) are a diverse group of carbon-based chemicals that easily evaporate into the air at room temperature. These compounds are emitted from a variety of sources, including vehicle exhaust, industrial processes, and natural sources like plants. While some VOCs are naturally occurring and essential for various biological processes, others are man-made and can have significant environmental and health impacts.

Minimum Sample Amount : 20g, 250ml
